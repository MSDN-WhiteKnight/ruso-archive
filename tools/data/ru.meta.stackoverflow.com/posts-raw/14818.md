<p>I appreciate all the users for expressing their opinions about what is best for the site. We are going to move forward with turning on Free Votes, as this change was chosen by the community on <a href="https://ru.meta.stackoverflow.com/q/14805/541910">the original poll</a>.</p>
<p>I understand that some users may not agree with the way the poll was conducted (in a post rather than an answer), but I actually think this is a simpler and clearer way to conduct a vote because there is only one place to express your assent or dissent.</p>
<p>On the unofficial poll here on this post some people voted (up or down) on the post itself, while others voted (up or down) on the answer that was intended to be the actual poll. Some users may have voted on both. Especially with the title of this post, I think it is likely that at least some users may have been confused about how/where to cast their vote.</p>
<p>Additionally we conducted these polls the same way (in a question post, not an answer) on all the sites where we have asked the community if they want to opt in to the new feature. I want to keep things consistent, and not have a different procedure on one site as opposed to the rest.</p>
